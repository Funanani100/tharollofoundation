About Us


THAROLLO FOUNDATION is an NPO formed to attend community issues especially in rural based communities. At Tharollo Foundation, we are dedicated to making a positive impact in our community and beyond. As a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO), our mission is to uplift and empower individuals, families, and communities through various initiatives and programs.

Amoung Our Objectives:-

We focus on education, healthcare, and sustainable development, we strive to create lasting change by addressing the root causes of social challenges. Through collaborative efforts, community engagement, and innovative solutions, we aim to build a brighter future for all.

Explore our website to learn more about our programs, events, impact, and ways to get involved. We welcome volunteers, donors,and supporters who share our passion for transforming lives and building a better tomorrow. Thank you for visiting Tharollo Foundation. Together, let's create a world of hope, opportunity, and empowerment.
